## Overview

| Aspect | ACP | UCP |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Led By** | OpenAI + Stripe | Shopify + Google + Walmart |
| **Focus** | Payment delegation | Full commerce interop |
| **Transport** | REST/HTTP | REST, MCP, A2A |
| **Scope** | Checkout & payment | Discovery to fulfillment |

## Philosophy Differences

### ACP Approach
- Payment-first design
- Tight Stripe integration
- Simpler scope, faster adoption

### UCP Approach
- Platform-agnostic
- Broader commerce coverage
- More complex, more flexible
